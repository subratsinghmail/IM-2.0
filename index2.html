VISION

  • The punchline of our company is "Augmenting farmers income through innovation and technology" , The Agriculture sector plays a vital role in the Indian Economy. Agriculture contributes about 16% of total GDP,farmers represent anywhere between 25%-50% contributing towards the backbone of an economy, however 83% of the freshwater goes in Agriculture. India exports a large quantity of agricultural materials like fruits, vegetables, pulses, tea, spices etc and the government is acquiring good revenue from it. According to the survey done, agricultural land in India increased from 59.7% to 60.4%.

  • Importance of agriculture in India was realised by everyone when Mr. Lal Bahadur Shastri, the second prime minister of India had given the slogan of ‘Jai Jwan Jai Kisan’. India is the country of farmers and orchards. Most of the people of India have adopted agriculture for their occupation. There are several acres of land which are used only for cultivation of different crops like tomato, potato, rice, wheat, sugarcane, apples etc. That is why agriculture is good to take as a profession.

  • Agriculture is also a source of employment. It is providing employment to many of the people. Many of the labourers get their wages daily through agriculture either they are tractor drivers, grass cutters, many technicians of farming apparatuses or everyone who indulge in such cultivation activities.
