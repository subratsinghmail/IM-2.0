Why Agriculture ?

  • The Agriculture sector plays a vital role in the Indian Economy.

    Agriculture contributes about 16% of total GDP,farmers represent anywhere between 25%-50% contributing towards the backbone of an economy, however 83% of the freshwater goes in Agriculture.

    India exports a large quantity of agricultural materials like fruits, vegetables, pulses, tea, spices etc and the government is acquiring good revenue from it. According to the survey done, agricultural land in India increased from 59.7% to 60.4%.

  • Importance of agriculture in India was realised by everyone when Mr. Lal Bahadur Shastri, the second prime minister of India had given the slogan of ‘Jai Jwan Jai Kisan’.

    India is the country of farmers and orchards. Most of the people in India have adopted agriculture for their occupation.

    There are several acres of land which are used only for cultivation of different crops like tomato, potato, rice, wheat, sugarcane, apples etc. That is why agriculture is good to take as a profession.

  • Agriculture is also considered as a source of employment. It is providing employment to many of the people.

    Many of the labourers get their wages daily through agriculture either they are tractor drivers, grass cutters, many technicians of farming apparatuses or everyone who indulge in such cultivation activities.

    Agriculture supplies raw materials to various agro-based industries like sugar, jute, cotton textile and vanaspati industries. Food processing industries are similarly dependent on agriculture.

  • It is important to create technologies and equipments which make farming easier and less of herculean task for the farmers.

    We at Innovative Machineries are constantly aiming to make equipments and machineries which meet the requirements for the farmers of the India.

Our Products

Rice Threshing Machine

A Threshing machine which works on Pneumatic technology

The main problem with the old traditional threshing method is that it is time consuming and requires a large labor. The burning of the paddy waste leads to smoke which results into accidents. The gases created by threshing machines causes an environmental problem. Rice Threshing machines are expensive and poor farmers cannot even afford it.

Our core Innovation is based on the Pneumatic Technology. We use Pneumatic Technology will be less harmful and will be easy to operate. Because Pneumatic technology uses air pressure to work. The machine has a distinct advantage as it provides good quality threshing. Since the paddy stem remains straight through the process, they can also be used to feed the cattles.
